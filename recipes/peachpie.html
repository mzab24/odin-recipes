Peach Pie

Description

This Peach Pie is the best summer dessert! It’s made with a buttery homemade pie crust filled with a sweet and saucy peach filling. Don’t forget the ice cream!

It’s made up of a simple Homemade Pie Crust and a four-ingredient peach filling. Simply whip up the pie pastry, add in the filling, choose between a lattice or a full top crust, then bake!

Ingredients

  • Pie Crust
  • Flour
  • Peaches
  • Cornstarch
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Prep the pie crust
  2. Make the filling
  3. Assemble the pie
  4. Bake
