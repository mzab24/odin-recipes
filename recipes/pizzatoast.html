This Pizza Toast is an easy dinner recipe hack made with store-bought garlic bread, pizza sauce, mozzarella cheese and your choice of toppings! An easy meal or snack for busy weeknights!

Ingredients

  • Store bought garlic bread
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  • Pepperoni and vegetables
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Toast garlic bread on both sides
  2. Spread pizza sauce on bread
  3. Top with cheese
  4. Add favorite toppings and bake
